Detail

Gently cleanse and nourish your skin with Venora Hydrating Milk Cleanser, a luxurious formula that removes impurities and makeup while delivering lasting hydration. Enriched with Shea Butter and Aloe Vera, this creamy milk cleanser softens and soothes the skin, leaving it supple, comfortable, and radiant. Ideal for dry and sensitive skin, it maintains your skin’s natural moisture balance and prepares it perfectly for serums and moisturizers.

Key benefits
  • Gently removes makeup and impurities
  • Hydrates and nourishes the skin
  • Soothes dryness and irritation
  • Maintains natural moisture balance
  • Leaves skin soft, smooth, and radiant
How to use

Apply a small amount onto dry or damp face and neck, gently massaging in circular motions. Wipe away with a damp cotton pad or rinse with lukewarm water. Use morning and evening for best results.

Ingredients

This creamy milk cleanser combines Shea Butter to deeply moisturize and soften the skin, Aloe Vera to soothe and calm, Glycerin to attract and retain moisture, Chamomile Extract to reduce redness and irritation, and Vitamin E to protect and nourish. Together, these ingredients cleanse gently while leaving the skin hydrated, soft, and comfortable.
