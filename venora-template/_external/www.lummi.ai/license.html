License

Everything you need to know about using our images freely.

What’s allowed

  • Use any image from Lummi for personal or commercial projects—no limits.
  • Make as many copies as you need, for yourself or your clients.
  • Share images wherever you like: websites, social media, eBooks, templates, and more.
  • Edit images however you want—tweak, combine, or remix them to fit your vision.

What’s not allowed:

  • You can’t re-sell Lummi images.
  • You can’t bundle our images to make your own stock image site or similar competing service.
  • You can’t say you own an image, even if you’ve edited it. The original artist still owns the work.

The long and short of it:

You don’t have to ask for permission or credit the creator or Lummi, though we always appreciate a shoutout. You get a free, forever license to use Lummi images however you like, except to create a similar competing service or to sell the images outright.
